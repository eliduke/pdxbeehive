The Fine Print

Outdoor Movies at The Beehive are fun and casual. We set up a projector in the backyard, lay out blankets, bust out some snacks and drinks, and the party starts itself. Sometimes we spark up a bonfire and sometimes it's just too fucking hot for that, am I right?!

Feel free to bring anything that you want to contribute: food, drinks, lawn chairs, blankets, and other friends! We'll have some of that stuff available, but if you're particular about something, make it happen. There will be some cooler space with ice for you to add cold drinks and a table or two for setting up snacks. Try your best to bring finger foods because who's got time to wash a bunch of plates and shit?
