The Right Fit

We're all pretty darn laid back and friendly if we don't say so ourselves. Some work early, others stays up late, but the house quiet hours are generally from about 8pm to 8am and everyone is very respectful of one another's needs, space, and schedules. Sometimes we have outdoor movie parties in the backyard, sometimes we just straight chill at home, and sometimes we're out partying until 4am last Friday night in our booty shorts!

We aren't a "chore wheel" kind of house, but we like tenants that take ownership of their space and invest a little in the everday goings-on. If something is a little dirty, clean it up! If something seems broken, try to fix it! In the very least, let others know so that we can all take care of each other. We don't do house meetings, but we end up hanging in the kitchen and catching up with each other pretty regularly. We also have a group text that we use for little updates, questions, and LOLz. We are an open communication kinda place. :)

We are looking for someone of any gender, any sexuality, any anything that wants to jump in and have fun with this.
